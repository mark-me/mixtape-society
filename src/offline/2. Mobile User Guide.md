# Mobile Mixtape Download Guide

## For Non-Technical Users

Your mixtape can now be downloaded and played on **any device** - phone, tablet, or computer. Here's how:

## üì± On iPhone (iOS)

### Option 1: Apple Music (Best for iPhone)

1. **Open your mixtape link** in Safari
2. Tap the **"Download"** button
3. Choose **"For Mobile Devices"**
4. Tap **"Open in Apple Music"** (or "Files" ‚Üí then open with Music app)
5. The playlist will appear in your Apple Music library
6. Tracks download automatically when you play them

**Note**: FLAC files are automatically converted to high-quality MP3 (320kbps)

### Option 2: VLC Player (Works offline instantly)

1. **Install VLC** from App Store (free)
2. Open your mixtape link in Safari
3. Tap **"Download"** ‚Üí **"For Mobile Devices"**
4. Choose **"Open in VLC"**
5. All tracks appear instantly - tap to play!

### What happens?
- The M3U file is a tiny playlist file (just text)
- When you tap a song, it streams from the server (or downloads)
- No huge ZIP files to extract on your phone!

## üîß Troubleshooting

### "I can't open the M3U file"
**iPhone**: Must use Safari browser (not Chrome)  
**Android**: Install VLC first, then try again

### "It says 'file not supported'"
You need a music player app:
- iPhone: VLC or Apple Music
- Android: VLC, Poweramp, or any music player

### "Nothing happens when I tap download"
Try:
1. Long-press the download link
2. Choose "Download linked file"
3. Then open from Downloads folder

### "Can I use Spotify?"
Not directly - Spotify doesn't support M3U imports for custom music. Use VLC instead (it's free!).

### "The files are huge!"
Use the Mobile option instead of Desktop ZIP. The mobile option streams on-demand, so you're not downloading everything at once.
