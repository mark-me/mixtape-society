## üì∫ Chromecast

Perfect for Android users and anyone with a Chromecast device.

### What You Can Do

- **Parties** ‚Äì Play through your home sound system
- **Background music** ‚Äì Stream to living room while entertaining
- **Shared listening** ‚Äì Enjoy together on the big screen
- **High-quality audio** ‚Äì Connect to premium speakers

### Requirements

Before you start, ensure you have:

- ‚úÖ **Chromecast device**
  - Chromecast (any generation)
  - Chromecast Audio
  - TV with Chromecast built-in
  - Speakers with Chromecast built-in

- ‚úÖ **Same WiFi network**
  - Phone/computer and Chromecast on same network
  - 2.4GHz or 5GHz WiFi works

- ‚úÖ **Modern web browser**
  - Chrome (recommended)
  - Edge
  - Opera
  - Firefox (limited support)

### How to Cast

#### Step-by-Step

1. **Open the mixtape** on your phone or computer
   - Click the shared link in your browser
   - Wait for player to load

2. **Look for the cast icon** (üì°)
   - Usually in player controls
   - Top-right corner or bottom controls
   - May need to click "More options" first

3. **Tap the cast button**
   - Browser shows available devices
   - Your Chromecast devices appear in list

4. **Choose your device**
   - Select from the list
   - Connection establishes (2-5 seconds)

5. **Start playing**
   - Tap play if not already playing
   - Audio streams to selected device
   - Video shows album art on TV (if casting to TV)

#### Quick Cast (Chrome)

**From Chrome browser:**

1. Right-click anywhere on page
2. Select **"Cast..."**
3. Choose your device
4. Page content appears on TV/speaker

### Controlling Playback

Once casting, you can control from multiple places:

**From the Mixtape Player:**

- ‚ñ∂Ô∏è Play/Pause
- ‚è≠Ô∏è Next/Previous
- üîä Volume control
- üìä Quality settings

**From Lock Screen (Mobile):**

- Full media controls appear
- Change tracks without unlocking
- See album art and track info
- Quick access to volume

**From Notification Shade (Android):**

- Pull down notifications
- Media controls always visible
- Quick pause/resume
- Stop casting button

**From Hardware Keys:**

- Media keys on keyboard
- Headphone controls (if supported)
- Car steering wheel controls (Android Auto)

### Stopping Chromecast

**To stop casting:**

1. Click the cast icon (üì°) again
2. Select **"Stop casting"**
3. Or disconnect from device list
4. Playback stops on device

**Or:**

- Close the browser tab
- Exit the application
- Select a different device

### Troubleshooting Chromecast

#### Device Not Appearing

**Solutions:**

1. Ensure same WiFi network
2. Restart Chromecast device
3. Restart router
4. Update Google Home app
5. Re-setup Chromecast

#### Connection Drops

**Solutions:**

1. Move router closer to Chromecast
2. Reduce WiFi interference
3. Restart Chromecast
4. Use 5GHz WiFi if available
5. Update Chromecast firmware

#### Audio Delay or Stuttering

**Solutions:**

1. Lower quality setting in player
2. Close other streaming apps
3. Restart router
4. Check for router firmware updates
5. Reduce WiFi congestion

#### No Sound

**Solutions:**

1. Check TV/speaker volume
2. Verify HDMI cable (for TV)
3. Test with different content
4. Restart Chromecast
5. Check audio output settings on TV

## üçé AirPlay (Apple Devices)

Native casting for iPhone, iPad, and Mac users‚Äîworks automatically!

### What You Can Do

- **Home audio** ‚Äì Stream to HomePod or Apple TV
- **Multi-room** ‚Äì Play on multiple AirPlay 2 speakers
- **Car** ‚Äì Use CarPlay in supported vehicles
- **Mac** ‚Äì Stream from desktop to any AirPlay device

### Requirements

- ‚úÖ **Safari browser** (required‚ÄîChrome won't work)
- ‚úÖ **Apple device**
  - iPhone (iOS 11.4+)
  - iPad (iOS 11.4+)
  - Mac (macOS 10.14+)

- ‚úÖ **AirPlay device**
  - Apple TV (3rd gen or later)
  - HomePod or HomePod mini
  - AirPort Express
  - AirPlay 2-enabled smart TVs
  - AirPlay 2-enabled speakers

- ‚úÖ **Same WiFi network** (device and AirPlay destination)

### How to Use AirPlay

#### From iPhone/iPad

1. **Open mixtape in Safari** (must be Safari)
2. **Start playing a track**
3. **Find AirPlay controls:**

   **Option A - From Player:**
   - Look for AirPlay icon in player controls
   - Tap to see available devices

   **Option B - From Control Center:**
   - Swipe down from top-right (iPhone X+)
   - Or swipe up from bottom (older iPhones)
   - Tap audio card in top-right
   - Tap AirPlay icon (‚ñ≤üì°)

   **Option C - From Lock Screen:**
   - Wake your phone (don't unlock)
   - See now playing card
   - Tap AirPlay icon

4. **Select your device**
   - Choose Apple TV, HomePod, etc.
   - Multiple devices for multi-room audio
   - Check devices to include them

5. **Start streaming**
   - Audio plays on selected device(s)
   - Control from any AirPlay interface

#### From Mac

1. **Open mixtape in Safari**
2. **Start playing**
3. **Click AirPlay icon** in menu bar
   - Or in Safari's audio controls
4. **Select device**
5. **Enjoy on speakers/TV**

### Where to Find AirPlay

**Control Center (iOS):**

- Quick access to AirPlay
- Change devices on the fly
- Adjust volume per device
- See what's playing where

**Lock Screen (iOS):**

- Full media controls
- AirPlay icon always visible
- No need to unlock
- Quick device switching

**Now Playing (iOS):**

- Tap playing card anywhere
- Expanded AirPlay controls
- Multi-room configuration
- Individual volume controls

**Menu Bar (Mac):**

- Persistent audio controls
- Quick AirPlay access
- System-wide availability

### AirPlay Features

**Multi-Room Audio:**

- Select multiple AirPlay 2 speakers
- Synchronized playback
- Individual volume control per room
- Add/remove rooms on the fly

**Handoff:**

- Start on iPhone, continue on Mac
- Seamless transition between devices
- Same account required

**CarPlay:**

- Automatic when connected to car
- Dashboard controls
- Siri voice control
- Safe, hands-free operation

### Controlling AirPlay Playback

**From iPhone/iPad:**

- üéµ Control Center ‚Äì Quick access
- üîí Lock screen ‚Äì Full controls
- ‚åö Apple Watch ‚Äì Wrist controls (if paired)
- üöó CarPlay ‚Äì Dashboard controls
- üéõÔ∏è Device itself ‚Äì Physical buttons

**From Mac:**

- ‚å®Ô∏è Media keys ‚Äì Hardware buttons
- üñ±Ô∏è Menu bar ‚Äì Quick access
- üéπ Touch Bar ‚Äì MacBook Pro controls

### Stopping AirPlay

**To stop streaming:**

1. Open AirPlay menu
2. Select **"iPhone"** or **"Mac"** (your device)
3. Audio returns to device speakers

**Or:**

- Disable AirPlay from Control Center
- Select different output device
- Close Safari tab

### Troubleshooting AirPlay

#### Device Not Appearing

**Solutions:**

1. Ensure same WiFi network
2. Restart AirPlay device
3. Restart iPhone/iPad/Mac
4. Update iOS/macOS
5. Check AirPlay device is powered on

#### Connection Issues

**Solutions:**

1. Toggle WiFi off and on
2. Forget and rejoin WiFi network
3. Restart router
4. Move closer to router
5. Update device firmware

#### Audio Cutting Out

**Solutions:**

1. Reduce distance between devices
2. Minimize WiFi interference
3. Close bandwidth-heavy apps
4. Restart router
5. Switch to 5GHz WiFi

#### Not Working in Chrome

**This is expected!** AirPlay requires Safari on iOS/Mac. Switch to Safari browser.

- **Voice control** ‚Äì Use Google Assistant
- **Safe operation** ‚Äì Designed for driving

### Requirements

- ‚úÖ **Android phone** (Android 6.0+)
- ‚úÖ **Car with Android Auto**
  - Built-in Android Auto
  - Or aftermarket head unit

- ‚úÖ **Connection method**
  - USB cable (recommended)
  - Wireless Android Auto (some cars)

- ‚úÖ **Android Auto app** (pre-installed on most phones)

### How to Use

1. **Connect phone to car**
   - USB cable to Android Auto port
   - Or pair via Bluetooth (wireless)

2. **Android Auto launches automatically**
   - Or tap Android Auto on car display

3. **Open your mixtape** in Chrome
   - Before or after connecting to car

4. **Start playing**
   - Mixtape appears in Android Auto music apps

5. **Control from dashboard**
   - Tap next/previous
   - Use steering wheel buttons
   - Voice commands: "Hey Google, next track"

### Safety Features

**While Driving:**

- ‚úÖ Large, simple controls
- ‚úÖ Voice command support
- ‚úÖ Minimal distraction
- ‚úÖ Glanceable information
- ‚úÖ Steering wheel integration

**What's Blocked:**

- ‚õî Complex interactions
- ‚õî Text input while moving
- ‚õî Detailed settings changes

### Voice Control

**Supported commands:**

- "Hey Google, play"
- "Hey Google, pause"
- "Hey Google, next track"
- "Hey Google, previous track"
- "Hey Google, stop"

### Troubleshooting Android Auto

#### Not Connecting

**Solutions:**

1. Check USB cable (try different cable)
2. Enable Android Auto in car settings
3. Update Android Auto app
4. Enable USB debugging (developer options)
5. Restart phone

#### Mixtape Not Appearing

**Solutions:**

1. Ensure mixtape is playing before connecting
2. Restart Android Auto
3. Update Chrome browser
4. Clear Chrome app cache
5. Reconnect to car

#### Audio Through Phone Instead of Car

**Solutions:**

1. Check car audio source (should be Android Auto)
2. Ensure USB cable carries audio
3. Adjust car volume
4. Restart Android Auto connection

## ‚ùì Common Questions

### Can I cast to multiple devices?

**Chromecast:** Yes, via Google Home speaker groups
**AirPlay:** Yes, AirPlay 2 supports multi-room
**Android Auto:** No, single car only

### Does casting use my battery?

**Less than direct playback!** Your device just controls the stream‚Äîthe receiver does the heavy lifting.

### Can others control while I'm casting?

**Chromecast:** Anyone on the same network can see and control
**AirPlay:** Only the device that started AirPlay
**Android Auto:** Only the connected phone

### What if I get a call while casting?

- Music pauses automatically
- Take your call
- Music resumes when call ends
- Casting stays connected

### Will casting work on mobile data?

**Not recommended.** Casting works best on WiFi. Mobile data can be slow and expensive for audio streaming.
